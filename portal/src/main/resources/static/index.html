About Us

Who we are?

The founding team comes from Speech Therapy background and has combined experience of more than 100 years of Therapy practice. As technology helped drive many businesses, we wanted to help the Therapy practice management area that did not have much options. We understand what it takes to build a easy to use therapy management solution that will help therapists.

What we do?

Besides providing cloud based therapy management solution, we provide services in Speech, Physical and Occupational therapy areas. We provide Early Intervention services that include evaluation, therapy and group programs. We are based out of Bay Area California and do cater services all around bay area. Universal Challenges

As a speech therapist, you want to spend as much time as possible with your adult and pediatric therapy patients… but there are so many day-to-day tasks that pull you away.

Document speech therapy sessions on the fly, right on your tablet.

Therapy EMR software pricing is based on a per-user basis. So if you had 5 users, the monthly fee would be $59.95 (1 user base package) plus $119.80 ($29.95 x 4 users) totaling $179.75.



A user is anyone that needs access to ClinicSource. Each therapist or provider will need a user account in ClinicSource. For example, if you had 15 therapists and 3 back office/administrative personnel, then you would need 18 user accounts.
