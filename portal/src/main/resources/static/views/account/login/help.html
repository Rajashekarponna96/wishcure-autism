How to change password

Step 1:

  • Navigate to 'Settings' 'Change Password'.

    • Step 2:

  • Provide your old password.

  • Provide your new password (Must contain an uppercase letter, lowercase letter, a number, a special character(! @ # $ * % &)).

  • Confirm your new password.
  • Click on submit to proceed

    How to manage the Appointment

    Step 1:

  • Navigate to Appointment. Add Appointment.


    • Step 2:

  • Provide start time, start date, end date, end time and Select the Department.

  • Click on 'Check Availability'.

  • Here you will get Therapist available Schedules.

  • Click on 'Book Appointment'.You will get Sub hours in a particular schedule. And Select the desired availability timings.

  • Click on desired availability timings. And you will be once again navigate to addAppointment page.

  • Fill up the required fields in form.

  • Click on "Submit" to save your Appointment details and you will be navigating to the Appointment list.


  • Then you can view the Appointment list and the Appointment details like,Therapist information and the Patient information with Time & date.

  • If Appointment completed, invoice will be generated.If you click on "invoice generated",you will be navigate to Invoice Page.

  • If you click on "Edit",you will be navigate to Patient Information Page.

  • If you click on "Eye",you will be navigate to Patient Information Page.

  • Here you can view the Generated Invoice and you can download it and at the same time you can take print of it.

  • Here you can access Patient Documents,Patient Goals Sheet,Patient Progress Notes,Patient Exit Note.

  • Here you can change the status of the appointment ,whether it is awaiting, completed or canceled.And Both Therapist and patient will get mail when the status is provided.

    How to manage the Patient Billing

    Step 1:

  • Navigate to Accounts. Patient Billing.


    • Step 2:

  • By default You can view the billing patients who are eligible for Billing .

  • you can also provide start date , end date and Select the Client type to filter the patients.

  • Click on 'Filter'. Then you will get all Patients between selected dates..

  • Click on 'Edit'. Then you will navigate to 'Patient Billing Details'.

  • Here you can see the Patient details and his Session details.

  • Here you can bill for the Patient by providing required fields.

  • Click on 'Submit'.your billing is completed.You will be navigate to Payments list.

  • Here you can see the Payment details of the Patient.

  • From Patient Billing page also you can navigate to this page by clicking on "Eye" button.

    How to manage Invoice

    Step 1:

  • Navigate to Accounts. Invoices.


    • Step 2:

  • By default You can view the all the Invoices .
